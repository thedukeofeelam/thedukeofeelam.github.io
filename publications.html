🔬 Current Research

Cross-National Mathematical Skills Inventory for Engineers

Collaborative research project with Cornell University aimed at designing and piloting a mathematical skills inventory for undergraduate engineering students.

Led 3 workshops at leading engineering universities, engaging educators from across the UK in primary, secondary, and higher education to examine and assess threshold concepts.
